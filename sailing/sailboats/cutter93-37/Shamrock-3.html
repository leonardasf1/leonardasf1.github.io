Shamrock III

Shamrock III

designed by William Fife
launched on March 17, 1903
built by William Denny and Brothers of Dumbarton, Scotland.

The yacht was designed by William Fife, and was built by William Denny and Brothers of Dumbarton, Scotland. The yacht was launched on March 17, 1903. It participated in the 1903 America's Cup and was defeated by the New York Yacht Club's Reliance in all three races on August 20, 1903; and August 25, 1903; and September 3, 1903. In 1920 the yacht was used for test racing against Shamrock IV and afterward was scrapped.
